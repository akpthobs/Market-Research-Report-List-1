<p><ul><li>Brushed</li><li>Brushless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC submersible pumps market is primarily divided into two types: brushed and brushless. Brushed pumps utilize a commutator and carbon brushes to transfer power, resulting in simpler designs and lower costs but higher wear and maintenance needs. In contrast, brushless pumps use electronic controllers for operation, enhancing efficiency, lifespan, and performance while reducing noise and maintenance. The choice between these types depends on applications, cost considerations, and the need for reliability in challenging environments.</p></p>
